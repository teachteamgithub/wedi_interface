Table @list:SPECIAL_DESIGNATION_LIST

DescriptionA list of student designations a district configures during set up. The delivery team will configure a maximum of 15 designations. There are 10 predefined designations that are the starting point when the tenant is being configured. The configuration list can be preloaded with the following values which will have SPECIAL_DESIGNATION_CD values of 01-10.

HANDICAPPED
HEARING_IMPAIRED
VISION_IMPAIRED
IEP
SPECIAL_ED
PLAN_504
FRMEAL

STUDENT_ELL
NEW_STUDENT
