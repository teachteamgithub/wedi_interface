Table STG_SIH_STUDENT_SERVICE_HOURS

DescriptionSource system data for student service hours. The data can come in as a block of time. For example, Michael submits 20 hours that he worked at the retirement home for the period from April 1, 2016 to May 31, 2016.

If the data is sourced a blocks of hours the student reports the ETL will have to write out a running total in the table. If the data is sourced as the current total for student within a title, for example 30 hours at Nursing Home the ETL will just write out the new total into the table.
