Table STG_SIH_ORGPERSONROLE

DescriptionThis table collects information about jobs or positions a person fills within a school district.

For example,
Jamie is a teacher at Coppell High School
Micheal is a student at New Tech Hight School
Jack is a principal at Coppell High School
Susan is the superintendant of Special Education for the Coppell School District

This table does not collect contact relationships.
