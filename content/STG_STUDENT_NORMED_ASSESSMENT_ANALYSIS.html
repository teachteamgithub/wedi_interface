Table STG_STUDENT_NORMED_ASSESSMENT_ANALYSIS

DescriptionFeed back on goal and proficiency attainment for an assessment written by a student. This is applicable only to Norm Assessments such as NWEA, SAT, ACT. For further clarification please reach out to the Watson Classroom Contact.
